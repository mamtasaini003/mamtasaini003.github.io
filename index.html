Hello, I am

Mamta Saini  

Scientific Machine Learning Engineer at Zenteiq Aitech, IISc Bangalore, India.

I am Mamta Saini, a Scientific Machine Learning Engineer at Zenteiq.ai (incubated at IISc Bangalore) and a Master’s student in Mathematics at NIT Kurukshetra. During my master’s, I started working with Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs), first applying them to classical PDEs and then extending them to fractional models using numerical schemes. This gave me confidence in combining mathematical techniques with machine learning to solve complex problems. Over time, my interest grew towards more advanced methods like variational PINNs and neural operators such as Fourier, Wavelet, and Geometry-Informed Neural Operators, which allow learning across different geometries and domains.

These experiences shaped my interest in Scientific Machine Learning, where I want to work on developing methods that are mathematically reliable yet efficient for real-world applications such as fluid dynamics, electromagnetics, and computational physics. Going forward, I aspire to pursue a PhD in this field, where I can focus on building scalable operator-learning frameworks and uncertainty quantification approaches that bring together theory and application.
